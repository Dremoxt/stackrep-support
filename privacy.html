Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Stackrep Fitness ("the App") is developed by Stackrep. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All fitness and exercise data you enter into the App is stored locally on your device.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional premium features through in-app purchases processed by Apple's App Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

Data Storage

All data created within the App, including your exercise records, categories, and settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since all data remains on-device and we collect no personal information from any user, the App is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
