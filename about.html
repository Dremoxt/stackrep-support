About Stackrep

A simple system for staying active — and an app to keep you honest

Stackrep is two things at once: a practical approach to fitting exercise into a busy life, and a free iPhone app that makes that approach effortless to follow.

The Idea

The problem with traditional fitness

Most fitness advice assumes you have a free hour, a gym nearby, and the energy at the end of the day to use both. For a lot of people — parents, full-time workers, anyone whose schedule isn't their own — that assumption breaks down quickly.

When the gym session can't happen, nothing happens. And when nothing happens often enough, the habit disappears entirely.

Stackrep starts from a different premise: you don't need an hour. You need two minutes, several times a day.

The System

Micro-workouts: small sets, spread across your day

The idea behind Stackrep is simple. Instead of one long workout, you do short bursts — a single set of an exercise — whenever you have a spare moment. Between meetings. Before breakfast. While the kettle boils. After putting the kids to bed.

Each individual set takes under two minutes. Accumulated across a full day, they add up to meaningful training volume — without ever requiring a dedicated block of time.

The system has three moving parts:

01 Pick 6–10 exercises
02 Set a daily set target
03 Tap whenever you finish a set

That's it. No program to follow, no rest days to schedule, no warm-up required. The exercises are yours to choose — from our library of 42 movements across 7 muscle groups, or any exercise you already know.

The goal isn't maximum performance. It's consistency. A moderate amount of movement every day, compounding over weeks and months, does more for your health and energy than an intense workout once a week.

The App

Stackrep on iPhone: one tap per set

The Stackrep app is the tracking layer for the system. It's designed to do exactly one thing with as little friction as possible: record that you did a set.

Open the app, tap the exercise you just did, close the app. The whole interaction takes under five seconds.

  • ⭕
    Progress ring A single ring fills as you hit sets throughout the day. When it's full, you've hit your target. Simple and satisfying.
  • 📅
    Consistency calendar Every day you hit your target turns a different color. Streaks and patterns emerge naturally — no charts to read, no analysis required.
  • 📊
    Exercise breakdown See which exercises you're hitting regularly and which you're neglecting. Weekly and monthly totals show your volume over time.
  • 🎯
    Flexible goals Set your own daily targets per exercise. Change them any time. There's no prescribed program to follow — you decide what "enough" looks like.
  • 🔓
    Completely free No subscription, no paywall, no ads. All features unlocked from day one. Your data stays on your device — no account required.
Who It's For

Not for everyone — and that's by design

Stackrep is not a tool for serious athletes, competitive lifters, or anyone following a structured training program. Those goals are better served by a coach, a gym, and purpose-built tracking software.

Stackrep is for people whose primary obstacle isn't motivation or knowledge — it's time and unpredictability. People who want to keep their body moving but can't reliably carve out an hour in their day. People who have tried gym memberships and quit because life kept getting in the way.

If your goal is simply to stay active, maintain some muscle, feel better, and build a habit that survives a chaotic schedule — Stackrep was built for you.
