About Stackrep

Stay active without finding time to work out

Stackrep is a simple system for fitting real movement into a busy day — one short set at a time. No gym, no hour-long session, no program to follow. Here's where to start.

A traditional workout is all-or-nothing: find the hour, or write off the day. Stackrep breaks that block apart. You do single sets in the gaps you already have — between meetings, while the kettle boils, after the kids are down — and they add up to real training over the day. The result is a habit that survives a schedule that won't sit still.

Who it's for

Stackrep is for people whose obstacle is time, not effort — parents, full-time workers, anyone with an unpredictable schedule. It's not built for competitive athletes chasing a performance target; that's a job for a structured program and a coach. If you just want to keep moving, hold some muscle, and feel better without fitness eating your day, you're in the right place.
