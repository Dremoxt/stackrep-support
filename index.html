The Philosophy

Movement is medicine. But going to the gym every day isn't realistic for most people. Life gets busy. Schedules change. Energy fluctuates.

Stackrep Fitness is built on a simple idea: consistency beats intensity. Instead of crushing yourself 3 times a week, do a little bit every day. 8 categories, a few sets each ‚Äî that's it.

Set different goals for different days. Take rest days when you need them. The app adapts to your life, not the other way around.
