About

Move Now helps you track your daily exercise sets across muscle groups, set weekly goals, and monitor your progress over time. Stay consistent and build healthy workout habits.

Features

  • Track sets for chest, back, shoulders, biceps, triceps, legs, abs, and cardio
  • Set daily and weekly workout goals
  • View your workout history on a calendar
  • Monthly stats and personal records
  • Dark mode support
