How It Works

Three steps. That's the whole system.

No periodization plans, no macros, no sleep scores. Just the three things that actually build a habit.

🏋️

Pick your exercises

Choose 6–10 movements that cover your whole body. Push, pull, squat, core. Use our exercise library or bring your own.

🎯

Set a daily target

Decide how many sets per exercise you want to hit each day. Start with 3–4. The app tracks your total and fills a progress ring.

👆

Tap every set, all day

Whenever you finish a set — between meetings, before breakfast, after the kids are down — open the app and tap. One tap, done.
